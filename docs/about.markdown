"Tibagan" is Tagalog for quarry (defined [here](https://www.collinsdictionary.com/us/dictionary/english/quarry)). I live in a house built on what used to be one, and there is still a large outcropping of limestone next to it.

My name is Raymundo Baquirin. I go by "raybaq" most places on the Internet and I have been a teacher and/or computer programmer most of my working years. I viewed my retirement as an opportunity to become a student again, but it turns out that being one while not being in school and having no pedagogue or taskmaster means that I am often not on task.

I think of this blog as a record of how I am learning, or attempting to learn, various things including how to become a lifelong learner. So far it feels like chipping away at a mountain.
