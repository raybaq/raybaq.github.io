First post--again, for the Nth time, once more!

I have started some sort of diary or journal or blog innumerable times over the decades, but I have high hopes that I'll stick with this one and write more than a post or two.

The thing is, I *have* been writing almost every day since January of last year; it's just been fairly disorganized and unpublished. So I think I have developed the habit of writing something everyday, and all I need to do is to keep it up.

A couple of weeks ago I started trying to get my writing organized; that involved revisiting [TiddlyWiki](https://tiddlywiki.com) which I was fascinated with long ago, and tried to use, but...without the commitment to writing something everyday no amount of fancy tooling will help, and as I said above it's only been a bit more than a year that I have been writing everyday. I'm using TiddlyWiki now as a journal which I back up [here on Github](https://raybaq.github.io/wiki-ni-ray/). I'm also reviving this blog. Github makes it easy to put something up without worrying about how it's going to look.

Not that I'll be posting everyday. Just when I've learned something new.

Till here and many thanks 8^)!
